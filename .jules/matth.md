## 2025-05-18 - [Lineage Coupling Analytical Reformulation]
**Learning:** Permutation testing for co-occurrences (e.g., cells of a specific clone appearing across multiple cell types) inherently mimics a multiple hypergeometric sampling process, drawing without replacement from fixed marginal label counts. Heuristic permutation testing introduces non-deterministic variation, consumes excessive compute, and poorly estimates extreme p-values due to limited iterations.
**Action:** Replace heuristic permutation tests for categorical co-occurrences without spatial dependencies with exact analytic formulations using the hypergeometric distribution and inclusion-exclusion principles to guarantee deterministic, stable statistical tests and achieve orders of magnitude faster execution.
