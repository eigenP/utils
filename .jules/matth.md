## 2025-05-24 - Exact Analytic Lineage Coupling
**Learning:** In `calculate_lineage_coupling` within `eigenp_utils/single_cell.py`, the previous implementation relied on a heuristic permutation test to calculate the expected overlap and variance of clones across different cell types. This approach was computationally expensive, non-deterministic, and relied on random shuffling.
**Action:** Replaced the permutation approach with an exact analytic formulation using the inclusion-exclusion principle and vectorized log-gamma approximation for the hypergeometric distribution (`scipy.special.gammaln`). By computing the exact expectation and variance of overlaps using combinations, we ensure correctness, eliminate non-determinism, and dramatically speed up computation, making the approach mathematically rigorous and parameter-free.
